Boats

Introduction to Boats:

A boat is a watercraft of a wide range of types and sizes, but generally smaller than a ship, which is distinguished by its larger size, shape, cargo or passenger capacity, or its ability to carry boats. Small boats are typically found on inland (lakes and rivers) or in protected coastal areas. However, some boats, such as whaleboats, were designed for operation from a ship in an offshore environment.
